What does it mean to sanitize links?

Sometimes links come with a bunch of extra garbage in them, mainly for analytics. Cleaning them up makes them look nicer, as well as removes the servers ability track who or where or how it was shared.

Can I sanitize links on my own?

Sure can!

9 times out of 10 you can remove everything after and including the question mark (?) in a url and it should work just fine.

If you need the question mark, junk between ampersands (&) may be removable.

Make sure your link still works before sending it off though!
