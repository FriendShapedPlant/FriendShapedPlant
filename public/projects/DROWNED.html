I AM WHAT YOU MAKE OF ME

She walks from the pond, hair drawn straight with water

hands and face too pale to be natural-

but regardless- they watched her drown!

the witch.
they watched her drown..

yet she stands.

A fucked up amalgam,

once a girl
she was drowned.

they called her a witch
and sunk her to the bottom of the lake.

There her story twisted,

in books

and tales

and newspapers

and gossip,

and with each retelling
she changed,

she was as they saw her,

no matter what she wanted.

She was always as they saw her.

She drowns in the lake

and twists
to become how she is remembered,

the girl she once was,
is gone

and all that remains
is her haunted form.

She haunts the lake she was once drowned in,

cursing those who come too close.

One girl,

returns each day,

for 3 seasons of a year

to swim in her lake,
for years
and years.

She resents this girl for invading her privacy so often

and thus curses her

for a year, haunting her every waking moment,

how does she like her space to be invaded, huh?
